Visualizing Weather

Fall 2016, Class Project
  • Design
  • JavaScript
  • Graphics
  • p5.js

This was done for my Information Design Class at MIT. The first challenge was to create a weather visualization that used no text or iconography. I decided to create a windmill whos wind speed and background is controlled by the weather outside my dorm. The next part was creating interesting charts from historical datasets.

All work is by Nina Lutz, unless otherwise noted. Contact directly for use of photos or code.
